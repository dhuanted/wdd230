Audience

The target audience is for those who live in Preston, Soda Spring and Fish Haven as well as nearby places and tourists. The demographic is made up of people between 20 to 65 years of age, with a taste for outdoor activities, gardening, sports and with technological devices so that they can consult the website and look up the weather forecasts for the next few days in order they can plan activities and clothing.



Scenarios

Can I find the weather forecast?
On natureish.net you can find the current weather and a forecast for the next 5 days.


How do I find out about sudden changes in the weather?
At natureish the weather is monitored and we post an emergency alert in case of sudden changes in the weather so you can take your precautions


Do you have weather images?
In our photo gallery you can consult the photos of the weather, taken by our professional photographers as well as the contributions section, with photos of our followers.
